Instructions

Confused on how to add new Tasks? Don't worry. Follow these steps to get your job done.

To add a "new task" in the TODO list just follow these easy steps:

  • Click on the Home button in the navigation bar to toggle to the Home page.
  • Click on the Search Box displaying the message "Add new task..."
  • Write down the task you would like to add in the list.
  • Click on the Add button.

Congratulations! You have just added a new task to your TODO List.
